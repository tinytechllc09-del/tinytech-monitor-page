Common Questions
Everything you need to know before getting started.

Will I notice anything different on my computer?

No. The monitor runs completely in the background as a silent Windows service. There are no popups, no windows, and no slowdowns. You will never know it's there.

What happens when you get an alert?

We contact you, explain what was found in plain English, and remote into your computer to fix it — usually before it becomes a real problem. Most issues are resolved the same day.

Is my personal data safe?

Yes. The monitor only watches for security threats — failed logins, suspicious programs, and unusual network activity. It does not read your files, emails, or personal documents.

What computers does it work on?

It works on Windows 10 and Windows 11 PCs and laptops. Linux (Ubuntu/Kubuntu) is also supported. Contact us if you're unsure about your setup.

We install the monitor at no charge and monitor your PC for 30 days. If you like it, we continue at your chosen plan rate. No credit card required to start.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes — no contracts, no cancellation fees. Just let us know and we'll remove the monitor the same day.
